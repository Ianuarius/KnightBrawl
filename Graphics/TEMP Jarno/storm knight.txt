the Storm Knight

Weapon: Lighting rod

Block: Creates 3 lighting spheres which reduces damage taken 25%. Deals damage to enemies.

Special 1: Sphere Barrage

Usable when you have 3 or none lighting spheres.
Creates 3 lighting spheres and then shoots all to enemy front of you.
Special 2: Shock Nova - Shocks every enemy around you making them vulnerable to incoming attacks.

Special 3: Thunder Cloud - Makes AoE lighting storm which hits rapidly to ground. It follows you.

Special 4: Lighting Ride - Warps to random location. Makes high damage upon arrival.
